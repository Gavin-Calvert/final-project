SUMMARY
The center of Persona 5's story hinges on its characters. Each of them have gone through their own struggles, all of which correlate with the main storyline. They are also all amazing.

CHARACTERS
Persona 5 starts you in a mysterious casino, with the mian character pulling off a heist, but getting arrested by the cops soon after. He is then taken into custody for questioning, and you spend most of the game before this point, trying to remember everything that you did.

LENGTH
Persona 5 is a very long game, with the majority of first playthroughs taking at least 90 hours to complete. Persona 5 removes htis issue by being consistently interesting throughout every moment.

CHOICE
Throughout the game, persoan 5 gives you many choices, particularly towards the ladder half of the game. Some choices won't affect gameplay at all, and some will completely change which ending of the game you get. Even with the less impactful choices, the player still feels like they did something that had a large effect
