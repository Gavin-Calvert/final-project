CORRUPTION
Persona 5 has you run into several corrupt adults that can truly be considered evil. Your goal in this game is to set justice and make things right

TOGETHERNESS
Persona 5 has a story that simply cannot be completed without companions. So much about the game revolves around the bonds you form with your friends and how it helps you through all your struggles. This is only emphasized by how spending time with them directly helps you in combat

BELIEVING
In persona 5, you summon your persona by tearing off a mask. This symbolizes how these characters awaken to their true power after taking their mask (literal and metaphorical) off and embracing their true selves

SPIRIT
This is an obvious theme if you've played the game. Personas are quite literally the manifestation of all of its users desires and heart. Only people with very strong wills can awaken to their personas, which reallly strengthens the characters of the people that do.
