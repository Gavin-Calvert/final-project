BASIC
Persona 5 has many unique ways to deal with enemies, but the best way to tear through them without wasting your resources is by using your standard attacks.

PERSONA
Persona attacks are the main source of dealing major damage in the game. These attacks come in a large variety of elements, with some being more effective against certain enemies than others. The way these attacks are balanced is with the mechanic of SP, limiting your uses of them, with your SP regenerating every time you leave the cognitive world

ITEM
Items are the most situational form of combat in persona 5, wich every combat-usable item being limited in use. These items, however, can be amazing in the right scenarios. Items can heal, do damage, apply and clear status effects, and much more

STRATEGY
A very important part of persona 5 is how you approach each battle. For some boss battles, it is highly beneficial to block a charge up attack to severely reduce damage taken. other times, it's a good way to skip a turn of a perty member. Orders are also helpful, with many of them affecting how you approach battles.
